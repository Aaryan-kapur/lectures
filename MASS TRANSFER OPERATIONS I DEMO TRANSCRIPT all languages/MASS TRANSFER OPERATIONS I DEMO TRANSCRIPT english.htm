Predictive equations basically in many situations we have experimental diffusivities values for binary gas mixtures and that are available in the literature, but still there are several mixtures for which experimental data are not reported. So, in this situation we can use suitable correlation to predict the value of diffusivity. These which values the predictive equations which are obtained from the predictive equation is based on some relations which is developed for different system based on the variation of the temperature pressure of the systems or the molecular weight of the components or their molecular volumes, so based on that these equations the predictive equations are proposed.

One of the empirical relations is Fuller equations.
